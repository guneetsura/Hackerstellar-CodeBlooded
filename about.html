The Chain Reaction

The Chain Reaction is a blockchain-based supply chain management application that provides real-time visibility and tracking of goods and products throughout the entire supply chain. It enables companies to query a product's status and location at any point in time and facilitates secure and transparent transactions between suppliers, manufacturers, and distributors through self-executing smart contracts. The app aims to improve efficiency, reduce costs, and provide transparency in the supply chain.
